Token Burning is a process in which a certain number of tokens are permanently destroyed, reducing the overall supply of tokens in circulation.

Staking is the process of holding a certain number of tokens in a cryptocurrency wallet for a specific period of time, in order to participate in the validation and security of a blockchain network
