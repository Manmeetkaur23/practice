FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Though Yoga was being practiced in the pre-Vedic period, the great SageMaharshi Patanjali systematized and codified the then existing practices of Yoga, its meaning and its related knowledge through his Yoga Sutras.

Along with being a way of life, it is also considered a practical science with five principles forming its core:
1.Proper Exercises (Asanas)
2.Proper Breathing (Pranayama)
3.Proper Relaxation.
4.Proper Diet and Nutrition.
5.Meditation and Positive thinking.

The six branches of yoga:
1.Raja yoga
2.Jnana yoga
3.Tantra yoga
4.Hatha yoga
5.Bhakti yoga
6.Karma yoga.
